3. Analysis on Air Quality - it's under development)
In this project, I aim to analyze and visualize pollution data by leveraging databases and APIs. My methodology involves several steps, from data collection to analysis and forecasting.

Project Overview for project №3:

Data Collection:
- I connect to various databases through APIs and collect data using SQL queries.
- I retrieve and parse data from web pages for further analysis.
  
Pollution Distribution:
- I display the distribution of pollutants over the years, showing trends and changes.
- I rank countries based on their pollutant concentration, identifying which nations contribute the most.
  
City Analysis:
- I identify cities within the top-ranked countries with the highest pollutant concentrations.
  
Pollutant Analysis:
- I examine each pollutant individually, analyzing their concentrations and interrelationships.
  
Country-Specific Analysis:
- For the top 10 countries with the highest pollution rankings, I upload additional data such as population, GDP per capita, quality of life, and total GDP to analyze potential correlations.
  
Seasonal Trends & Forecasting:
- I explore whether seasonal trends exist in pollution levels.
- I also aim to predict future pollution levels for a selected country using statistical modeling.
